Sad Fat Cat

Tuesday September 3rd, 2024


In the corner sits a lonely cat

Eyes of sorrow, fur of matte


A world once bright now fades to gray

Where sunlight's warmth has gone away


The fat cat sits by the window sighing deep

With heavy paws and eye that weep


He dreams of chasing micec in fields far away


But days drag on in a lazy endless loop

No more leaps just quiet lonely stoop


A shadow of the cat he used to be

Longing for the days he used to be free.
